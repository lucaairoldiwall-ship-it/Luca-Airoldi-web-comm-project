Members

Who are the M(atteo) A(lessandro) L(una) L(uca) S(ergio) ?

Matteo — Vocals

Frontman

Despite having a shy nature, his presence on the stage is unmatched. He will make you stand from your seats and you'll find yourself singing along with him.

Alessandro — Guitar

Lead Guitar

Technical riffs and jaw-dropping solos, what more do you want from a lead guitarist? Maybe Alessandro's charming charisma.

Luna — Guitar

Rythim Guitar

Don't be fooled by her small figure, Luna brings a massive sound like few other guitarists do. Also, her icecold glaze is a warning! This kitty has fangs!

Luca — Bass

Groove

A true hardcore bass player, with a natural groove and plenty of style. Despite fitting perfectly in the punk genre, he also brings some funk into the mix.

Sergio — Drums

Engine

A talentuos multi-instrumentalist that can basically play every thing which he puts his hands on, but his true talent comes out on the drumkit. Let him comb your hair with his infinite energy!
